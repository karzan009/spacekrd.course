Lehmann Maupin Art Gallery :

    Some informations :

  • Founded in 1996: Lehmann Maupin is a contemporary art gallery established in New York City by David Maupin.
  • Global presence: It currently has four locations worldwide, in New York, Hong Kong, Seoul, and London.
  • Focus on established artists: The gallery represents renowned artists like Kader Attia, Erwin Wurm, and Gilbert & George.
  • Significant influence: Lehmann Maupin actively contributes to museum shows, public projects, biennials, art fairs, and online platforms, demonstrating its strong influence in the art world.
  • Large space and staff: The gallery boasts a sizeable space of around 2,000 square meters and employs a team of approximately 45 staff.
  • Highly regarded: Lehmann Maupin is considered one of the most influential contemporary art galleries, earning it a well-deserved spot on your list.
