Victoria Miro Art Gallery :

    Some informations :

  • Victoria Miro Gallery opened its first space in London in 1985.
  • They moved to a larger space in 2000, which remains their main London office.
  • A second London space opened in 2013.
  • A third gallery opened in Venice, Italy in 2013.
  • Victoria Miro and her gallery are highly respected in the art world.
  • As of 2018, they had three gallery spaces covering 2,000 square feet with around 50 staff.
  • Currently, they have two active galleries, one in London and one in Venice.
  • The gallery represents and has launched many prominent artists, including Chantal Joffe, Chris Ofili, Njideka Akunyili Crosby, Tal R, Alice Neel, and Idris Khan.
