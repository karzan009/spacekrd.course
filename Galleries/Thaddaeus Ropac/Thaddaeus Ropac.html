Thaddaeus Ropac Art Gallery :

    Here are some key points :

    Established and expanding:

  • Founded in 1981, the gallery has grown to six locations across London, Paris, Salzburg, and Seoul.

    Curates influential artists:

  • Represents significant figures like Georg Baselitz, Adrian Ghenie, Gilbert & George, and more.
  • Collaborates with estates of recently deceased artists to preserve their legacy.

    Market focus and activity:

  • Operates in both primary and secondary markets.
  • Participates in major art fairs, curates diverse exhibitions, and runs its own publishing house.

    Global reach and impact:

  • Occupies 12,000 square meters of exhibition space.
  • Consults for museums and public collections worldwide.
  • Actively contributes to the discourse surrounding contemporary art.

    Highly ranked:

  • Recognized as one of the best art galleries globally, earning a well-deserved seventh place on the list.

    • Overall, the information aligns with Thaddaeus Ropac's position as a major player in the contemporary art world.
