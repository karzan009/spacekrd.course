Pace Art Gallery :

    Strengths :

  • Global reach:
    • With nine locations across North America, Europe, and Asia, Pace has a significant international presence.
  • Impressive size and scale:
    • Their exhibition space of over 15,000 square meters and sizable staff of 150 further solidify their position as a major player.
  • High-caliber artist roster:
    • Representing renowned artists like Christo, Jeff Koons, David Hockney, and Agnes Martin, Pace boasts a prestigious portfolio.
  • Strong exhibition program:
    • Their impressive exhibition calendar showcasing major artists demonstrates their commitment to presenting impactful artworks.

    Points to consider :

  • Ranking as third biggest:
    • While Pace's size and reach are impressive, some sources may rank other galleries like Hauser & Wirth or David Zwirner above them based on different criteria, such as total sales or square footage per location.
  • Seasonal and temporary locations:
    • While their planned expansion in London is promising, it's important to note that certain locations like East Hampton and Palm Beach are temporary or seasonal, possibly impacting the overall square footage compared to galleries with permanent spaces.
