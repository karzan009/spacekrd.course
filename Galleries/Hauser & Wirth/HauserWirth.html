Hauser & Wirth Art Gallery :

    Impressive Expansion and Reach :

  • Founded in 1992, Hauser & Wirth has impressively grown from a single Zurich location to 15 global galleries across North America, Europe, and Asia.
  • With two galleries in New York and Somerset, their presence in major art hubs reinforces their international influence.

    Unique Approach to Location and Architecture :

  • Hauser & Wirth's commitment to restoring historical buildings and integrating contemporary art within cultural heritage sites sets them apart.
  • This approach adds a unique dimension to their galleries, connecting the present with the past.

    Academic Engagement and High-Quality Exhibitions :

  • Hauser & Wirth actively contributes to academic research, publishing, and organizing landmark exhibitions with renowned curators.
  • This dedication to scholarship and presentation elevates their program to museum-quality standards.

    Renowned Artist Roster :

  • Representing over 80 established and emerging artists, including Berlinde De Bruyckere, Nicole Eisenman, and the estates of Louise Bourgeois and Philip Guston, Hauser & Wirth boasts an extraordinary portfolio.

    Blurring the Lines Between Gallery and Museum :

  • With their impressive scale, academic engagement, and commitment to high-quality art experiences, Hauser & Wirth arguably bridges the gap between gallery and museum.
  • This innovative approach justifies their position as a true mega-gallery and secures their second place ranking.

    • Hauser & Wirth's combination of extensive global reach, unique architectural vision, academic engagement, and exceptional artist representation solidifies their position as one of the leading art institutions in the world.
